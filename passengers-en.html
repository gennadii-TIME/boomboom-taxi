Passenger information

Request a ride, compare driver offers, and choose the best. Early stage payments are cashless.

How to request a ride FAQ

What matters

  • Passengers see price upfront—no surprises.
  • Pick a driver by rating, price, and car.
  • Route changes? The system updates cost.

How to request a ride

  • Add route and pickup time.
  • Get driver offers with price and ETA.
  • Pick the best offer and confirm.

How to choose a driver

  • Check rating, car, and experience.
  • Choose by price: driver sets the fare.
  • Every 100th ride is free (sometimes 98th or 101st).

Payment

At launch we only support cashless payments. Cash and crypto will follow.

  • Cards and Apple/Google Pay.
  • Receipts and history inside the app.
  • Route changes recalculate the cost.

Safety

  • You can block drivers for 1 week / 1 month / 6 months / 1 year / forever.
  • Drivers see your rating and trip history.
  • Support responds 24/7.

FAQ

  • How to cancel? Cancellation policy is shown before confirmation.
  • Driver late? Switch to another offer.
  • Are taxes included? Yes, all fees are included in the total.
