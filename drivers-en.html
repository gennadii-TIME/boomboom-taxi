Driver information

Quick parameters

  • Default fare: €0.50 / km and €0.10 / min
  • Change price even while working

How it works

  • Set your fare in the app.
  • Requests come with your pricing.
  • You have 30 seconds to accept.
  • After confirmation, complete the ride.

Your price

Change pricing anytime. Distance and time are included; tolls and waiting are listed separately.

  • Base fare: €0.50 / km, €0.10 / min.
  • Adjust fares freely to fit the situation.
  • Passengers see cost before confirming.

Commission & rating

  • Higher rating = lower commission.
  • Skipping 3 requests in a row lowers rating by 0.1.
  • Trip and payout history always available.

Documents

  • Driving license, insurance, vehicle registration.
  • Photo of the car and plate.
  • Bank account confirmation for payouts.

Safety

  • Block passengers for 1 week / 1 month / 6 months / 1 year / forever.
  • Passengers must follow behavior rules.
  • Support responds 24/7 to driver issues.

FAQ

  • Can the route change? Yes, the system recalculates price.
  • How to get paid? At launch cashless only; cash and crypto will be added.
  • Passenger no-show? Cancellation and waiting rules are in the app.
