Service Rules & Disclaimer

BoomBoom Taxi Service Rules

Terms for drivers and passengers, payment rules, rating, and privacy.

General terms

  • BoomBoom Taxi is a ride-matching platform; it is not a carrier.
  • Using the service means you agree to rules and privacy policy.
  • The platform may update rules with notice to users.

Driver responsibilities

  • Provide valid documents and keep the car safe.
  • Follow the agreed route and inform about changes.
  • Respect passengers and follow safety rules.

Passenger responsibilities

  • Provide accurate route and be on time for pickup.
  • Follow behavior rules and do not distract the driver.
  • Follow payment instructions and respect the vehicle.

Platform responsibilities

  • The platform provides infrastructure and support but is not liable for user actions.
  • BoomBoom Taxi can suspend access for rule violations.
  • In disputes the platform helps collect information.

Payment rules

  • At launch only cashless payments are allowed.
  • Route changes recalculate the total.
  • Commission is charged automatically; drivers receive the agreed amount.

Rating system & blocking

  • Rating affects driver commission and request priority.
  • Mutual blocks: 1 week / 1 month / 6 months / 1 year / forever.
  • Skipping 3 requests in a row decreases rating by 0.1.

Data and privacy

  • Personal data is used to operate the service and keep users safe.
  • Data may be shared with partners only legally and to deliver services.
  • Users can request deletion or correction of their data.
