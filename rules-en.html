BoomBoom Taxi rules

Clear conditions for drivers and riders. Ratings, discipline, and protection keep trips fair and safe.

Fare and price

  • The driver sets the fare and can change it anytime.
  • The rider sees the final price before confirming.
  • If the route changes, the cost is recalculated automatically.

Rating and discipline

  • Confirming late for 3 trips in a row lowers the rating by 0.1.
  • Steady work and quick responses raise the driver rating.
  • Passenger violations are recorded; the driver can request a block.

Payments and commission

  • Cards, cash, and crypto are accepted.
  • Commission is auto-deducted, drivers receive the net amount.
  • Receipts and trip history are available to both sides.

Safety

  • Driver documents and cars are verified.
  • Support assists in conflicts or emergencies.
  • Passenger blocks: 1 week, 1 month, 6 months, 1 year, or permanent.

Responsibility

  • Drivers must follow traffic laws and platform rules.
  • Riders must respect the car and the driver's time.
  • Any fraud results in account blocking.
