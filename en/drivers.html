BoomBoom Taxi for drivers

In BoomBoom Taxi, the driver sets their own price and controls their earnings. Commission goes down when your rating and tenure grow. Passengers see your experience, car, rating, and terms.

Your rate Set the price per km/min before going online — and change it at any time.
30-second response Each request must be accepted or declined within 30 seconds. Three skips in a row mean -0.1 to rating.
Transparent commission The commission decreases with a high rating and longer tenure.
Experience Passengers see how long you have been on the service and trips completed.
Flexible route Routes can change on the way with passenger confirmation.
Payments Cash, bank card, or cryptocurrency.
